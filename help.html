Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my shipment?

You can track your shipment using the tracking number on the homepage or track.html.

How do I schedule a pickup?

Log into your FedEx account or visit your local FedEx office to schedule a package pickup.

What should I do if my package is delayed?

Contact FedEx support or check the latest tracking updates. Weather or customs can cause delays.

How can I contact customer service?

You can contact us directly at the bottom of this page or through the Contact Us section.
